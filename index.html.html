How It Works

Understanding Amazon
Dropshipping

A legitimate, scalable, and fully manageable business model — when operated with the right systems, compliance, and expertise.

Traditional Dropshipping

In the traditional model, when a customer places an order, the seller purchases the product from a supplier who ships it directly to the customer — no inventory required, no warehousing costs.

While straightforward, traditional dropshipping often lacks the operational structure, supplier reliability, and platform compliance needed for long-term stability.

  • No upfront inventory investment
  • Supplier ships directly to customer
  • Seller manages orders and payments
  • Margin depends on supplier pricing

Amazon Dropshipping
— The Prime Scale Way

When a customer places an order on Amazon, we purchase the product from approved suppliers or retailers and fulfill the order directly to the customer — while maintaining complete operational systems, order management, tracking, customer support, and scalability infrastructure.

Our approach is compliance-first, built for long-term operations, and designed to scale with your goals — not against Amazon's policies.

  • Safe, compliant supplier networks
  • Full order and tracking management
  • Automated workflows for scale
  • Customer satisfaction systems
  • Risk management protocols
  • Continuous supplier optimization
  • Policy-compliant operations
  • Performance monitoring

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your Amazon journey with Prime Scale Agency.



Still Have Questions?
Do I need prior Amazon experience to work with you? +
Not at all. We take care of everything from account creation to full-scale operations. Many of our most successful clients had zero Amazon experience when they started. We guide you through every step of the process and handle the technical complexity entirely.
Who owns the Amazon account and store? +
You do — 100%. Your Amazon seller account is registered in your name, and you have full access at all times. We operate as your management team, not as account owners. Your store is your asset.
How does the profit-share model work exactly? +
Our compensation is structured as a share of your store's profit rather than a flat agency fee. The exact percentage is determined during your strategy call based on budget, goals, and business model. This aligns our incentives: we only earn well when your store performs well.
How much capital do I need to get started? +
Capital requirements vary based on the service model — Amazon Dropshipping requires less upfront than Wholesale or Private Label. We'll assess your financial situation during our consultation and recommend the best starting point. Our credit utilization strategies also help clients scale without requiring massive immediate capital outlays.
Is Amazon dropshipping legal and compliant? +
Yes — when operated correctly. Amazon allows dropshipping under specific conditions: you must be the seller of record, fulfill through approved suppliers (not other retailers), and ensure all documentation reflects your store. We build our operations around these guidelines with strict compliance protocols at every level.
How long before I start seeing revenue? +
Most clients see their first sales within 2–4 weeks of launch. Meaningful revenue typically develops over 2–4 months as products gain traction, reviews accumulate, and PPC campaigns optimize. Scaling to consistent 5- and 6-figure revenue is a 6–12 month process depending on the model and investment level.
Do you work with clients outside the US? +
Absolutely. We work with clients globally — we've served entrepreneurs from the US, UK, UAE, Canada, Australia, and many more countries. Our team operates remotely and supports clients across all time zones. We'll guide you through the US Amazon marketplace setup regardless of your location.
What if my account gets suspended? +
We have dedicated account reinstatement specialists on the team. If an issue arises, we handle the appeal process, root cause analysis, and communication with Amazon Seller Support — working to restore your account as quickly as possible. Our compliance-first approach is designed to minimize the risk of suspension from the start.
